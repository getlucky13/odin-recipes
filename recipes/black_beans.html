Pressure Cooker Black Beans
Description

This is a great recipe for black beans that can be used alone or added to so many more recipes.

  • Prep: 10 Mins
  • Cook: >40 Mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 Servings
Ingredients
  • 7 cups water
  • 1 pound dry black beans
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro, tied together with butcher twine
  • 1.5 teaspons salt
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
Steps
  1. Combine water, black beans, onions, cilantro, olive oil, and salt together in pressure cooker.
  2. Cover and seal.
  3. Cook on high according to manufactures instructions, about 40 minutes.
  4. Allow steam to release completely.
  5. Remove lid and stir balsamic vinegar into beans.
